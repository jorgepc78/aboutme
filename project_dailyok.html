Daily OK

This project came up due to the pandemic and the necessity of having tools that could help small organizations with health-status online forms. The idea was to create an app that receives a notification asking for filling out an online form with some health questions on a daily basis. Based on the answer, the app shows a status in colors that reflects the situation of the user.

  • Framework / language in Back-end : Loopback (NodeJs framework)
  • Framework / language in Mobile : Ionic 5
  • Database: PostgreSQL
  • Admin Platform: Angular 12
  • Hosting: AWS BeansTalk
  • Development year : 2021
  • Development time : 6 months
